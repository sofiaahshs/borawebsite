TOURISM



Jawili Falls

There are seven cascades at Jawili Falls, all of which flow into swimming-friendly rock pools. The area has been developed with tourists in mind; there are showers and a modest restaurant accessible.



Bakhawan Ecopark

The 1.3-kilometer track will take you deep into the forest. Various types of mangrove trees will be seen as you journey towards the end. You could also try riding a bamboo raft or kayak above the waters of the Eco-Park.



Hurom-Hurom Cold Spring Resort

While being a pool, the main pool is actually a basin that collects water from a mountain spring. Being far less expensive than traveling all the way to Boracay, locals prefer taking a dip here. The water is really cold, which is ideal for summer.



Hinugtan Beach Resort

A private beach resort called Hinugtan Beach Resort is found in Buruanga, Aklan, which is 45 minutes by boat from Boracay. For those seeking for a day trip to a deserted beach, it is now open to the public and is now accessible for Getaway tours from Boracay.



Boracay

Boracay offers an abundant array of thrilling activities, including swimming, island hopping in the Philippines, diving, snorkeling, parasailing, kayaking, dining, and parties. Because of these, Boracay has consistently been listed in reliable publications' and travelers' choice lists as one of the world's top islands and beaches.



Hacienda Maria

In Buruanga, Aklan, there is a heritage and eco-tourism destination called Hacienda Maria. Its 14 hectares include a variety of natural beauties, including two caves with stalactite and stalagmite formations. A hike up to a waterfall just on property may also be taken by those who want to cool down.



Nabas Wind Farm

The Nabas Wind Farm in Aklan now provides clean and renewable energy to the Western Visayas grid on a regular basis, because of its 18 massive wind turbines that have also become tourist attractions in the previously quiet village. The Nabas Wind Farm is the province's most expensive project, costing much more than Php 4 billion, and is located in Barangay Pawa, some 25 kilometers from the renowned Boracay beaches.

PROVINCE MISSION AND VISION


Vision

Progressive and Sustainable Aklan

Mission

To realize its vision, the province shall ensure and support, among other things:

  • Preservation, enrichment and promotion of arts, culture and tourism;
  • Promotion of health, social services and non-handicapping environment;
  • Enhancement of the right of the people to a balanced ecology;
  • Encouragement and support to the development of appropriate and self-reliant, scientific and technological capabilities;
  • Enhancement of moral values and public accountabilities;
  • Enhancement of economic prosperity, food security and social justice;
  • Promotion of equal employment and livelihood opportunities among the residents;
  • Enhancement of Disaster Risk Reduction and Management System and increase adaptive capacity measures to the effects of Global Warming resulting to climate change;
  • Maintenance of peace and order; and
  • Improve the living conditions of the people of Aklan.
